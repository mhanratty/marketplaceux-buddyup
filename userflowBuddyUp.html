User Flow for BuddyUp app

This was the ideal flow for the BuddyUp app. What was implemented in protype was different. See notes below.


Notes

  1. Prototype did not have invite sending. Instead a persistent "Start"/"End" button appeared at the top of the chat.
  2. Visual design of Helper app was different.
  3. There was no dedicated return to chat button in the Helper interface.
  4. Ending the chat did not send a survey to the person being helped.
