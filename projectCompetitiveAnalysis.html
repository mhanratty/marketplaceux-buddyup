Amazon Mayday

How we can differentiate

  • Allow users to get help from friends and family rather than or in addition to support agents.
  • Provide support via volunteer community members who are compensated with data credits or other rewards and recognition.
  • Open up the ability to screen share and remotely control the mobile device to use cases beyond just live help, for example, co-browsing photos or playing a game together.
  • Allow helper to remotely control the device in addition to annotating on screen.
