Other mobile Live Help solutions

Amazon Mayday

  • Flow - on Kindle or Amazon Fire Phone user taps on mayday button in quick settings and a FaceTime-like call is initiated with Amazon service person (article)
  • Experience - Agent can annotate screen but cannot control the user's device expect to move the FaceTime-like video box
  • Response Time - Time to connect to a representative is 9.57 seconds (article)
  • 75% of questions from Kindle Fire customers come in through Mayday
  • Other Use Cases - Amazon agents have been asked questions beyond support including getting through an Angry Birds level, 109 requests from customers needing assistance with ordering a pizza, 3 customer requests for a bedtime story (article)
  • Availability - Amazon Fire Phone only available in US with one carrier (AT&T)
    • Problematic when fastest growing segment of cell phone market is non-premium market (article)
    • Amazon is in talks with several European companies, including Vodafone of Britain and Telef√≥nica of Spain to sell the phone in Europe (article)
  • Impact on Sales - Unclear if Mayday has helped the tablet's sales. Amazon's share of the tablet market was down by 1.8 percent, to 4.8 percent, over the last year (article)

How we can differentiate

  • Allow users to get help from friends and family rather than or in addition to support agents.
  • Provide support via volunteer community members who are compensated with data credits or other rewards and recognition.
  • Open up the ability to screen share and remotely control the mobile device to use cases beyond just live help, for example, co-browsing photos or playing a game together.
  • Allow helper to remotely control the device in addition to annotating on screen.
